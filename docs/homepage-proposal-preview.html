The story in three moments

01 · Arrival

A branded splash and centred logo set a calm, boutique tone before the customer reaches the hero — closer to walking into Conduit Street than landing on a catalogue page.

02 · Discovery

Summer heroes, curated editions, and product rails tell the same Jovoy story with more breathing room — swipe on mobile, hover depth on desktop.

03 · Connection

Search and Contact are no longer afterthoughts. Search expands with intent-led suggestions; Contact opens an elegant in-page preview with store details and next steps — without leaving the homepage.
